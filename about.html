About Revoton Precision

Manufacturing excellence in precision turned components for heavy equipment — driven by Industry 4.0 techniques and B2B partnerships.

Our Story

Founded on engineering rigor, Revoton Precision specializes in producing high-precision pins, bushes and related turned components for heavy earth moving machinery (HEMM). We combine skilled workforce with automated CNC production cells and digital quality tracking to deliver traceable, consistent parts.

Mission

To be the trusted B2B supplier of precision components for OEMs and distributors — delivering consistent quality, batch traceability and scalable manufacturing.

Certifications & Capabilities

  • ISO 9001-aligned quality systems
  • CNC turning & grinding cells
  • In-line metrology & SPC
  • Surface treatments & coatings
  • Custom drawing-to-part services
